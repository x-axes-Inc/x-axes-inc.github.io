THE COSMORAMA

In an era of ongoing climate risks, shifting demographics and political upheavels, no single discipline can bring about a paradigmatic change.
Urban planning and design has become a highly interdisciplinary field of expertise.
Urban designers should be those who reimagine the world's cities from a holistic perspective, those who reconfigure resource allocation in a more equal and efficient way, and those who conceptualize social-environmental justice into spatial interventions and design practices.
